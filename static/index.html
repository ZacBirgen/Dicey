Instructions

Welcome! This is a Dungeons and Dragons ability score generator. It will generate 6 random stats by rolling 4d6 and dropping the lowest number. If you check the box labled "ReRoll 1s" all 1s rolled will be rerolled. In addition, you can click the indivdual numbers to reroll them.
Have fun with your new characters!
